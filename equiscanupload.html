HOW TO USE

  1. Download and print out the Equiscan template and fill in your test answers to create a key.
  2. We strongly recommend downloading a free app called GeniusScan for this next step because it will strongly improve the accuracy of your results
  3. Using GeniusScan, click the plus sign in the bottom left corner and take a picture by aligned the camera with the edges of your Equiscan key template
  4. Once the picture of the key is taken, click Done and wait for your image to load
  5. Click the tinker button on the bottom right of the screen, crop the image if needed, and place the Black & White filter on the image.
  6. Then, click the share button, ensure that the format is a JPEG, and email the image to yourself.
  7. From your laptop, upload the JPEG image of your test key written on the Equiscan template into the Upload section below using your email.
  8. Following steps 3 through 6, upload JPEG images of your students answers on the Equiscan templates by copying and pasting them all at once into the Upload section below.
  9. Click start scanning.
  10. Watch as your papers are graded effortlessly!
