HOW DO I USE EQUISCAN?

  1. Download and print out the Equiscan template and fill in your test answers to create a key.
  2. We strongly recommend using a scanning app for this next step because it will create a cleaner image and strongly improve the accuracy of your results.
  3. Take a photo of your key and upload the JPEG image into the Upload section below (note: ensure the image is fairly lit, cropped so the there is no background, and has no shadows).
  4. Then, upload JPEG images of your students answers on the Equiscan templates just as you did with the key and paste them all at once into the second Upload section below.
  5. Click start scanning.
  6. Watch as your papers are graded effortlessly!
