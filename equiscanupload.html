HOW TO USE

  1. Download the Equiscan template and fill in your test answers to create a key.
  2. Upload a jpg/jpeg image of your test key written on the Equiscan template (ensure that the lighting is appropriate, there are no shadows, and there is a non-white background).
  3. Upload jpg/jpeg images of your students answers on the Equiscan templates by copying and pasting them all at once into the file upload.
  4. Click start scanning.
  5. Watch as your papers are graded effortlessly!
